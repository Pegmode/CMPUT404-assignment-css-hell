Who am I?

My name is Daniel Chu and I am a Chiptune artist, Computer Science student, vintage computer enthusiast from Edmonton Alberta Canada. I am currently majoring in Computer Science at the University of Alberta. My hobbys are programming and writing music for vinatge computers and video game consoles.

Development History

I have worked on various programs and tools for retro computer music development. Of note I am the developer of the open source Game Boy sound engine and exporter for a commercial music tracker called Deflemask.

What I have to offer

I have experience in file format reverse engineering, low level programming, retro assembly development and retro video game music.
